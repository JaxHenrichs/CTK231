R.E.P.O. Companion App

A mobile app design that serves as a quick reference guide for gameplay. Allowing players to learn more about the monsters, and the best ways to combat them. Players can also research what the best items are in the game, allowing easier progression. The app also serves as a lore guide, allowing players to learn more about the mysterious world of R.E.P.O. and the story behind it.

REPO Player Retention

A quest system designed to improve retention by introducing structured gameplay loops and rewarding progression. It addresses the issue of unstructured exploration by giving players clear goals, encouraging continued engagement, and providing a more purposeful gameplay experience, while still preserving the game’s open-ended exploration and core gameplay loops.
